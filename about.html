About Me

Hi, my name is Russell Randall and I'm obsessed with helping health and fitness freaks like myself start, persivere, grow and to look and feel good while becoming the healthy person you want to be that, I believe, provides true freedom to live a longer and happier life.

How did I start doing this?

I started running in 2008. I decided to start getting thinner the way 90% of others are, and that is by being inspired by seeing fit, healthy, happy and good looking people all around me, on TV and on magazines.

I will say that I don't compete in events. Perhaps I might one day. I don't do fun runs and I have no plans of running marathons. Don't get me wrong. If that is for you that is great and if that inspires you keep it up. I get it, that's great but it's just not for me.

I only ever started with one mission in mind and it's the same reason now. I was overweight and uncomfortable and one day I said enough was enough and I didn't care what it took but I was going to take control of my body and mould it, style it and shape it exactly how I want. And that still stands today. That is my inspiration, that is my reason, and it's even more of a reason now I have this site as this will keep me even more in check and strict to show you what is possible when you put your mind to it

I'm not competing, I don't want to win any races. I'm not out to prove anything. I just want to Feel good and look good running. Now I do, and I can show you how too.

No matter what your goals, chosen sport or hobby, looking good makes you feel good and when you feel good you will continue to enjoy it and more likely to stick with it. That is it. Look kick ass and you will kick ass!

So that is what I am about. Dress for the occasion in the most kick ass gear you like and you'll feel great about being out and doing it. Enjoy what you do and you'll do it more. Simple as that.
